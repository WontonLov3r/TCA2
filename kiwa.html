Kiwaaaaa or the Yeti Crabbbb

  • The yeti crab farms its own food – on its arms. The hairy ‘arms’ of this tiny crab capture all kinds of bacteria that lives and grows on the crab

  • The deep-sea habitat of the yeti crab has had an impact on the development of the creature

  • The yeti crab official name comes from mythology. The Polynesian goddess of shellfish was named Kiwa
